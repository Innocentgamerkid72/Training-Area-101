How to Play

Quick rundown before you start

  • Fill every row, column, and 3×3 box with the digits 1–9, each appearing exactly once.
  • Tap a cell, then tap a number (or press a key 1–9) to fill it in — wrong entries flash red and cost a few points, but won't end the game.
  • Toggle Notes mode to jot down candidate digits in a cell without committing to one yet.
  • Stuck? Use Hint to reveal a cell's correct digit — it costs more points than a mistake, so use it sparingly.
