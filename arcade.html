How to Play

Quick rundown before you start

  • A color word appears, but its ink color is never what the word says.
  • Watch the two lights above the word. When COLOR lights up, answer with the ink color. When TEXT lights up instead, answer with what the word says — ignore the ink.
  • Click, tap, or press 1-5 for your answer before the timer bar runs out, or it counts as a miss.
  • Correct answers score points and log your reaction time; wrong answers cost points.
