How to Play

Quick rundown before you start

  • Pieces fall automatically — arrange them to fill a complete horizontal row, which clears it and scores points.
  • Move with ← → (or A/D), rotate with ↑ (or W), soft-drop with ↓ (or S), and hard-drop instantly with Space.
  • Clearing multiple rows at once (up to 4, a "Tetris") scores far more than clearing them one at a time.
  • Every 10 lines cleared raises your level, which speeds up the drop rate. The game ends when the stack reaches the top.
