How to Play

Quick rundown before you start

  • Standard chess rules — tap a piece to select it, legal moves highlight on the board.
  • Tap a highlighted square to move there. Green dots are quiet moves, red rings are captures.
  • Castling, en passant, and pawn promotion are all supported automatically — promoting lets you choose the piece.
  • Checkmate ends the game in a win; if the side to move has no legal moves and isn't in check, it's a stalemate draw.
