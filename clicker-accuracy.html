How to Play

Quick rundown before you start

  • Targets will appear based on your settings — click them quickly and accurately.
  • Watch for Don't Shoot targets (marked with a red X) — hitting one costs you points instead of earning them.
  • Letting a target expire counts as a miss, but won't cost you points the way a wrong click does.
  • Your final score depends on hits, misses, and any penalties from Don't Shoot targets.
